01 Framing the Problem

5W1H: Defining the design challenge

Who

Remote team leads, PMs, and consultants in 6+ meetings per day

What

An AI meeting assistant that transcribes, summarizes, and integrates with team tools

When

As remote work becomes permanent and meeting overload grows across industries

Where

Web marketing site + SaaS product, used on desktop during and after meetings

Why

72% funnel drop-off. Users could not differentiate Fivo from 5+ competitors in under 5 seconds

How

Integration-first messaging, trust-building through security signals, and role-specific use cases
